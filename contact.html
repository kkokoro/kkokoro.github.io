About Me

Hello, I'm Morgan!

I’m a Computer Science student at the University of North Carolina at Charlotte, studying AI, Robotics, and Game Development, with a strong interest in the artistic and creative side of games. I enjoy exploring character design, 2D/3D art, and immersive environment creation, with a focus on how visuals support storytelling and player experience.

I have experience using tools such as Unity, Blender, and Fusion, along with a broad programming background that includes Java, Python, C, and additional languages gained through coursework and projects. Through collaborative and individual projects, I work on character design, concept art, and world-building, taking ideas from early sketches to in-game assets.

My goal is to continue to grow as a game artist and designer, combining technical knowledge with creative skills to help build engaging, well-crafted games. In addition to development and art, I have a growing interest in translation and localization, and how adapting language and cultural context can enhance storytelling and accessibility in games.

Outside of development, I enjoy playing games of all genres, reading, learning new languages, hiking for inspiration, and exploring storytelling, art, and design in games, film, and other media. These interests inspire the ideas, characters, and worlds I create in my projects.
