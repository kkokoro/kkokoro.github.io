About Me

Hello, I'm Morgan!

I’m a Computer Science student at the University of North Carolina at Charlotte, studying AI, Robotics, and Game Development, with a passion for the artistic side of games. I enjoy exploring character design, 2D/3D art, and immersive environments, focusing on how visuals enhance storytelling and player experience.

I have experience with Unity, Blender, and Fusion, and a strong programming foundation in Java, Python, C, and other languages. In both team and individual projects, I create characters, concept art, and game worlds, turning ideas from sketches into playable, polished assets.

My goal is to grow as a game artist and designer, combining technical skills with my creativity to create engaging, well-made games. I’m also interested in translation and localization, and how adapting language and cultural context can improve storytelling and accessibility.

Outside of development, I enjoy gaming, reading, learning languages, hiking, and exploring storytelling, art, and design across games, film, and other media. These interests inspire the characters, worlds, and ideas I bring to my projects.
