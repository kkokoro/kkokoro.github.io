About Me

I’m a senior Computer Science student at UNC Charlotte, with a focus on Game Development, AI, and Robotics. I’m especially passionate about the artistic side of game creation; character design, worldbuilding and storytelling through visuals that brings a game's atmosphere and narrative to life.

Right now I work with Unity, Fusion, and Blender to bring ideas to life, working across 2D/3D art, character design, and environment modeling. I also have experience programming in Python, Java, C, and C++, which helps me understand how art connects with gameplay and how to bring ideas into an actual game engine.

Outside of development, I enjoy learning and exploring new creative outlets like sketching characters, studying art styles, language learning, and finding inspiration in nature. These experiences broaden my persepective and influence the way I design characters, environements and the worlds they belong to.

My goal is to grow as a game artist and designer and to create work that is visually expressive, thoughtfully made, and connected to the ideas and expereinces that inspire me most.
