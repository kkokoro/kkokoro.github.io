Process & Sketches

My process usually starts with rough sketches and broad shapes, then moves into refining structure, lighting, and color. I like building things up in layers and adjusting as ideas change or new details appear.

  • Exploring silhouettes and shapes
  • Building values and mood
  • Experimenting with color and details
  • Preparing pieces for use in games or other projects

This area will eventually include sketch pages, progress shots, and breakdowns so you can see how pieces grow from basic ideas to more finished artwork.

About & Contact

I'm a computer science student focusing on game development and the artistic side of interactive worlds. I enjoy building characters, environments, and stories that could live inside a game or animated project.

This portfolio is a work in progress, and I'll keep updating it with new concepts, studies, and finished pieces as I create them.
