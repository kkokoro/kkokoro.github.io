Process & Sketches

For me, character and environment art is all about storytelling. I start with broad shapes and gestures, then refine them based on how they would exist in a game world; how they move, what they care about, and how their surroundings shape them.

  • Silhouette + shape language to define personality and role
  • Value studies to lock in mood and readability
  • Color experiments based on emotion, time of day, or game moment
  • Translating designs into game-ready assets when possible

About Me

I'm a computer science student focusing on game development and the artistic side of interactive worlds. I enjoy building characters, environments, and stories that could live inside a game or animated series.

This portfolio is a work in progress — I'll keep adding new concepts, studies, and finished pieces as I go.
