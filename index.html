Process & Sketches

My process usually starts with rough sketches and broad shapes, then moves into refining structure, lighting, and color. I build things up in layers and adjust as ideas change.

  • Exploring silhouettes and shapes
  • Building values and mood
  • Experimenting with color and details
  • Preparing pieces for games or other projects

This section will eventually include sketch pages, progress shots, and breakdowns so you can see how pieces grow from basic ideas to more finished artwork.
